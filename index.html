What's Cooking

Global Donut is a gallery of open-source software experiments at the intersection of social network health and community mental wellbeing — built by and for the decentralized web development community.

These tools are shaped by real-world use, released the moment they're useful, and totally free to take, fork, or ignore. Some will change your life. Some might only work for one person on the planet. That's fine.

Current experiments involve 🪟 window.ai 🌐 Chrome Origin Trials 🔗 local-first PWAs 🕸️ social graph tools — all aimed at giving communities better software mirrors of their own health.

House Philosophy

🌞 Ship first. A working thing in someone's hands beats a perfect thing in a drawer.

🌱 Give it away. We build to share, not to capture. If it helps one person, it's worth it.

🍩 Embrace the hole. Social network health is as much about what's missing as what's there. Gaps are data.

🔗 Decentralized by default. Your community's data belongs to your community.
